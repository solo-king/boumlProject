Relation P (<directional composition>)

the predicated covariance matrix is made of 4 3x3 sub-matrices and it is
semi-definite positive.

P = | P00 P10 | = | P00 P10 |
| P01 P11 | | P10t P11 |

Since P01 = transpose(P10), the code below never calculates or
stores P01.
